About the Book

PLAGUED follows Dash Blake and his loyal rabbit Gubby as they navigate swarming insects, deadly traps, and dark secrets in a race to survive and save what matters most.

Main Characters

  • Dash Blake – Arrogant Olympian, obsessed with winning.
  • Ivy Atropa – Mysterious vet, connected to the plague.
  • Gubby – Dash's prized white rabbit.

Blurb

"Winning is good. However, Dash Blake, Australia's star Olympian runner, is losing something worse than a race. His journey takes him to places where things aren’t what they seem, and the cure to his loss is in riddles. Dash and Ivy must journey through swarms of bugs to reach the cure. There is always a price to pay."
